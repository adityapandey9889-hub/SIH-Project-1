Our Mission

At ZeroWasteX, we're not just managing waste – we're transforming it into valuable resources that power sustainable communities across India.

Our comprehensive waste management platform connects citizens, authorities, and recycling centers through innovative technology, creating a circular economy where nothing goes to waste. Through Smart India Hackathon, we've developed a solution that makes waste management efficient, transparent, and rewarding for everyone involved.
