‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions

What is ZeroWasteX and how does it work? +

ZeroWasteX is a comprehensive waste management platform that connects citizens, waste collectors, and recycling centers. We provide door-to-door collection, smart segregation, and convert waste into valuable resources while rewarding users for eco-friendly actions.

Which areas do you currently serve? +

We currently operate in 45+ communities across Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, and Chennai. We're rapidly expanding to new areas. Check our service availability on the homepage or contact support for specific locations.

How much does the service cost? +

Our basic waste collection service is free for all users. Premium features like express pickup and detailed analytics are available through subscription plans starting at ‚Çπ99/month. Many services actually reward you with points!

What types of waste do you accept? +

We accept all types of household waste including organic waste, recyclables (paper, plastic, glass, metal), e-waste, and hazardous materials. Each category has specific collection and processing methods to maximize recycling and minimize environmental impact.

How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is simple! Click "Sign Up" on our homepage, enter your mobile number, verify with OTP, and complete your profile with basic information and address details. You'll be ready to start using our services immediately.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it? +

On the login page, click "Forgot Password", enter your registered email or mobile number, and you'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password. If you need help, contact our support team.

What are your collection schedules? +

Collection schedules vary by area and waste type. Typically: Organic waste (daily), Recyclables (3x/week), E-waste (on-demand), Hazardous (monthly). You can view your personalized schedule in the mobile app or website dashboard.

What if I miss my scheduled pickup? +

Don't worry! You can request an express pickup through the app for a small fee, or wait for the next scheduled collection. For premium users, we offer flexible pickup times and missed collection recovery at no extra cost.

How do I earn points? +

Earn points by: Proper waste segregation (+50 pts), On-time collection participation (+25 pts), Completing training modules (+100 pts), Referring friends (+200 pts), Participating in community events (+150 pts), and maintaining daily streaks (2x multiplier).

How can I redeem my points? +

Visit the Rewards section to browse our catalog. Redeem points for shopping vouchers, eco-friendly products, experiences, or donate to environmental causes. Points never expire and can be combined for bigger rewards.

The mobile app is not working properly. What should I do? +

Try these steps: 1) Force close and restart the app, 2) Check for app updates in your app store, 3) Clear app cache in settings, 4) Restart your device, 5) Ensure stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact technical support with your device details.

Can I use ZeroWasteX without a smartphone? +

Yes! You can access all services through our website on any device with internet. You can also call our support hotline for assistance with scheduling, account management, and service requests. However, the mobile app provides the best experience.
